Overview

Hourlance: Simple time track is a time-tracking app designed to help users log work sessions, manage projects, review reports, and unlock premium features through auto-renewing subscriptions.

The app is designed so that your project and time-entry content is primarily stored on your device. Some limited technical and usage information may also be collected to understand product usage, improve the app, and measure subscription-related behavior.

Short version: your tracked work data is mainly used to operate the app for you, while analytics data is used to understand app usage and improve the product.

Information We Collect

Information you create in the app

  • Project names, icons, colors, billing flags, and optional auto-stop settings.
  • Time-entry information such as start time, end time, billable status, and optional notes.
  • App preferences, such as watch auto-sync and widget refresh settings.

Subscription information

  • Whether you have free or Pro access.
  • The active subscription plan and expiration or renewal-related status made available through Apple StoreKit.
  • Purchase, restore, and entitlement status events needed to manage premium access.

Usage and device information

  • App screen views and feature interactions, such as opening reports, viewing the paywall, starting or stopping a timer, creating projects, or restoring purchases.
  • Technical information used for analytics and app performance, which may include device type, operating system, app version, coarse region, app instance identifiers, and similar diagnostic or usage metadata.
  • Deep-link usage if you trigger app actions through supported app URLs.

Information we do not ask you to create

The app does not require you to create a direct account with us to use its core features.

How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core time-tracking, reporting, widget, and watch-related features of the app.
  • To determine whether free or Pro features should be available on your device.
  • To understand which parts of the app are used most often and where users encounter friction.
  • To measure subscription funnel events, such as paywall views, plan selection, purchase starts, completed purchases, cancelled purchases, and restore flows.
  • To improve the app's usability, reliability, and feature planning.
  • To respond to support or feedback requests you choose to submit.

Firebase Analytics

Hourlance: Simple time track uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to understand product usage and improve the app. Firebase Analytics may collect information such as app interactions, approximate location derived from IP address, device and app identifiers, device model, operating system, app version, and engagement events.

In Hourlance: Simple time track, analytics events may include actions such as app launch, tab changes, timer starts and stops, manual entry creation, project creation or deletion, report navigation, paywall views, subscription events, and settings changes.

We use this information to analyze behavior trends, understand feature usage, and improve conversion and retention. We do not use Firebase Analytics data to serve third-party ads inside the app.

For more information about how Google handles Firebase data, please review Google's materials at https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Subscriptions and Payments

Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple may provide us with subscription status information necessary to confirm purchases, restores, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and entitlement changes.

Subscription management, billing terms, and payment handling are also subject to Apple's own terms, privacy practices, and App Store policies.

How Information Is Shared

Within your Apple device environment

The app may share limited app data locally between the main app, widget, and watch-related features using Apple's app-group and platform mechanisms so those features remain in sync on your devices.

With service providers

We may share limited technical and usage data with service providers that help us operate the app, such as Firebase Analytics.

For legal or security reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, security, or the integrity of the app.

No sale of personal information

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

Your project and time-tracking content generally remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or remove the app and its local data.

Analytics and subscription-related technical data may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary for product analysis, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or operational records, subject to the retention settings and policies of our service providers.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including rights to request access, correction, deletion, or information about how your data is used.

  • You can delete projects and time entries from within the app.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple App Store account settings.
  • You can contact us using the support link below for privacy-related questions or requests.

If you submit a privacy request, we may need information sufficient to verify and process that request where required by law.

Children's Privacy

Hourlance: Simple time track is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the situation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice inside the app or through the App Store listing.
